UX Research & Design Portfolio

Queensborough Community College – QCC Connect App

🚀 Final Product

The QCC Connect app was transformed into a user-friendly, responsive tool tailored to the needs of students navigating college life.

  • 🌙 Introduced Dark Mode for accessibility
  • 🚌 Launched Real-time Transit Tracker
  • 📍 Interactive Campus Map for navigation
  • 🔐 Biometric Sign-in for quick access
  • 🗓️ Redesigned “My Schedule” for clarity

🧪 Usability Testing

We tested clickable prototypes with real QCC students and staff.

  • Tested with 15 participants
  • Captured usability metrics (task time, error rate, satisfaction)
  • Insights: users preferred simple layout, disliked loading times

💡 Result: Simplified the schedule view and added a loading indicator.

🧠 Design Ideation

After identifying pain points, we brainstormed several features:

  • Low-contrast redesign to support dark mode
  • Modular card system for dynamic scheduling
  • “Quick Access” dashboard idea to cut down tap-throughs

Tools: Figma, Adobe XD, Paper Sketches

📐 Prototyping & Wireframes

We built low-fidelity and high-fidelity prototypes to explore flow and layout:

  • Used Figma for collaborative prototyping
  • Tested swipe, button, and tab patterns
  • Incorporated microinteractions and loading states

🔍 Discovery & Research

Understanding the needs of the QCC community was the foundation of this project. I organized a full-scale research initiative to gather diverse perspectives:

Recruitment Strategy

Recognizing the importance of broad representation, I proactively recruited classmates across different majors — including Nursing, Engineering, Liberal Arts, and Business — ensuring we captured a wide range of user needs and behaviors.

  • 📚 Personally invited 30+ classmates across multiple disciplines to participate in surveys and interviews
  • 📄 Designed and posted over 50 flyers across campus (library, cafeteria, student union, computer labs)
  • 🎯 Flyers included QR codes leading directly to screener surveys to streamline recruitment

Collaboration with Marketing Department

To scale up the reach, I partnered with the QCC Marketing Department. Working together, we:

  • 🎥 Created short promotional slides featuring a QR code linking to the screener surveys
  • 📺 Displayed the QR code on digital signage screens across the campus (e.g., student center, lobbies, cafeterias)
  • 📈 Increased survey participation by 70% within the first week of promotion

Research Methods

Through a combination of qualitative and quantitative methods, we gathered insights:

  • 📝 110 survey responses collected within two weeks
  • 🎙️ 15 structured user interviews (average 20 minutes each)
  • 🖥️ 10 usability testing sessions using mid-fidelity prototypes
  • 🔎 Analyzed app store reviews for supplemental insights into user frustrations

🎯 Key Problem Identified: "I can't find my classes easily or know when the bus is coming."

💬 Secondary Insights: Students wanted faster access to schedules, campus navigation, and real-time transit updates — all in a simple, reliable app experience.
